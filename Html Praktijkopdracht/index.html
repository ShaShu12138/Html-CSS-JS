Details

Our farm is located in southern China, where there is plenty of sunshine and a beautiful environment. Our orange variety comes from Brazil and is one of the best in the world. We ensure that consumers can enjoy the highest quality orange juice.

More than 90% of the year in direct sunlight makes for our best oranges. Every orange is carefully selected, leaving only the best fruit for our consumers.

The oranges are processed layer by layer and finally delivered to you, like a glass of freshly squeezed orange juice. Our products are rich in pulp, and in one bite, the aroma of oranges spreads in your mouth, which is even better than freshly squeezed orange juice!
