We care about

Minute Maid is committed to exploring efficient recycling, recycling and public education of beverage bottles. Leveraging the capabilities of the Minute Maid system, Minute Maid unites all sectors of society to explore effective solutions to ensure a sustainable future for the environment and the planet.

In 2021, The Minute Maid released a new global water strategy for 2030, bringing positive changes to people, ecosystems and companies. Up to now, Minute Maid has promoted more than 50 water resources protection projects, including water source protection plans, sustainable agriculture demonstrations, wetland protection and restoration, and farmhouse constructed wetlands.

In 2013, Minute Maid, in conjunction with the One Foundation, local government agencies, front-line social organizations and other national rescue partners, established a "24-hour water purification" emergency rescue mechanism, with the purpose of delivering urgently needed safe drinking water to disaster-stricken areas.
